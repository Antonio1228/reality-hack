# Reality Hack Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Reality Hack is an AI-driven alternate reality game that generates short, safe real-world missions.

## Data Stored on Your Device

Reality Hack stores mission history, mission notes, and optional evidence photos on your device by default. These local records are used to show your archive and personalize the in-app experience.

You can delete local app data from the Control tab.

## Data Sent for AI Generation

When you request a mission, the app may send a short mission-generation summary to a Supabase Edge Function, which calls OpenAI on the server. This summary may include:

- Player alias
- Broad location hint
- Mission time preference
- Story mood and safety level
- Previous mission title
- Last mission feedback
- Low-risk system context, such as hour, weekday, color scheme, battery level, network type, locale, and permission state

The app is designed not to send raw photos, full photo libraries, raw microphone recordings, contacts, private documents, or precise location history to the AI service.

OpenAI API Platform inputs and outputs are not used to train models by default. See OpenAI Enterprise Privacy: https://openai.com/enterprise-privacy/

## Permissions

Reality Hack may request access to camera, microphone, photos, location, motion, and notifications. These permissions are optional and are requested only when a feature needs them.

You can change app permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

## Purchases

Reality Hack uses Apple In-App Purchase and RevenueCat to process and verify Pro access. Purchase processing is handled by Apple. RevenueCat may process purchase entitlement data needed to unlock paid features.

## Third-Party Services

Reality Hack may use:

- Supabase, for server functions and optional backend services
- OpenAI, for AI text generation
- Apple, for App Store distribution, platform permissions, and in-app purchases
- RevenueCat, for purchase entitlement verification

## Safety

Reality Hack should never require dangerous, illegal, humiliating, or privacy-invasive actions. Any mission can be skipped.

Reality Hack does not provide medical, psychological, legal, financial, or emergency advice.
